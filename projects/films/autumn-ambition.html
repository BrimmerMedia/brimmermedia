AUTUMN AMBITION

FILM BY DEVIN BRIMMER


Autumn Ambition tells the story of a high school senior who faces the difficult decision of what to do after high school. During this short, the teen takes a bike ride to reflect on his past troubles, and ultimately make a life-changing decision. This film was entirely created by BrimmerMedia. Directing, writing, editing, shooting footage, and graphic design work was all handled by Devin Brimmer of BrimmerMedia. Film serves as an excellent medium for narrative storytelling, allowing for complete creative expression of thoughts and ideas. Please be sure to watch our short film at the YouTube page above.

WCF COMMERCIAL

FILM BY DEVIN BRIMMER


The Washington County Fair in Upstate New York is the event of the summer! The family friendly fair supports youth and the local community in many ways. It is filled with agriculture, carnival games, demolition derbies, tractor pulls, and much more! Our commercial was created to highlight the exciting events of the fair. It is set to air during either the summer of 2020 or 2021. Bringing an audience to local businesses and events has been a top priority at BrimmerMedia. Please be sure to watch the commercial at the YouTube page above.

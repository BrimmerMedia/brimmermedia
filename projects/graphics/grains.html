GRAINS EATERY

BRANDING BY DEVIN BRIMMER


This is a fictitious logo for use in the previously mentioned “Digital Prepress” project. Grains was created with the main focus of making and selling bread. Two wheat ears surround this logo to stress its organic nature. The script font and light minimal font stress the elegance of this brand. Their slogan “premium bread” is showcased in how high class this logo makes them appear.
As a continuation of the previous logo project, our “Digital Prepress” instructor assigned a project in which we were to create a magazine spread which highlights a special feature of our brand. I chose to promote the fact that Grains was now offering baking lessons in their store. The same colors and design elements from the logo of the company were incorporated in this spread. The background features wheat and bread, quickly emphasizing the purpose of the editorial and drawing a viewer in to read more.

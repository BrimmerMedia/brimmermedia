ACS THEATER

LOGO BY DEVIN BRIMMER


Adirondack Center Stage is a theater company located in the Adirondack mountains which approached my BOCES class for a logo. Each student created a logo for the company and they chose my design which fit their brand the best. I incorporated the two most important aspects which define their brand: theater and location. Their unique location in the Adirondacks was shown using mountains at the top of the logo. These mountains fade into a spotlight which stresses their theater background. The text at the bottom is also met with accent lines to replicate a stage. This logo has since become the icon for Adirondack Center Stage.

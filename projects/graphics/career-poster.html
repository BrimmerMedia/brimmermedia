CAREER POSTER

POSTER BY DEVIN BRIMMER


This poster was created to advertise the career of film directing. During Graphics and Communications, students were assigned to pick a career which interests them and create a poster to bring it to young students’ attention. This design incorporates elements and facts related to being a movie director. While using alignment an emphasis, the camera was placed to draw the viewers eyes to the facts of the career after noticing the “Movie Director” title. Each piece of information included would be important to a young audience.

AN AUTUMN RIDE

EDITORIALS BY DEVIN BRIMMER


During “Introduction to College Writing,” each student was assigned to write a short story. My short story resembled the narrative of my short film’s story. My “Graphics and Communications” class then assigned me to take my story and turn it into two different magazine editorials. I wanted to promote fall colors so I used a vibrant orange tone on all photography and design elements. On my second editorial I also made a path throughout the layout as if you were following the character on his bike ride.

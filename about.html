DEVIN BRIMMER

FILMMAKER & DESIGNER


Devin Brimmer is both a filmmaker and graphic designer. He has worked with many businesses and helped create their brand identity using various media. His company, BrimmerMedia focuses on designing and creating advertising materials for various companies. We also create and assist in the creation of narrative short films. Film and Graphic Design are our specialties. Our founder and Creative Director, Devin Brimmer, strives to provide both great service and great products. During the launch of a company, branding is everything. How your business is received by an average audience can quickly determine your success. BrimmerMedia is here to streamline that process and help you promote your business flawlessly. We will create commercials, logos, advertisements, editorials, product designs, and much more.

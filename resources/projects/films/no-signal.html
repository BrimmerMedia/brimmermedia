NO SIGNAL

FILM BY DEVIN BRIMMER


NO SIGNAL follows a young boy named Casey navigating through his crumbling home life in the mid-2000s. Casey comes home from one rainy day at school to an empty house. We watch as he makes his own meals and completes his homework in this sterile environment. While at his desk completing his homework, his absent parents finally arrive home arguing with each other. Casey turns on his old CRT TV and watches a sitcom to drown out the loud sounds of fighting. He dreams of himself being in the sitcom where there is a healthy family dynamic.
